Delicious Pasta Recipe

How to Make Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large pot with salt and olive oil.
  2. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions.
  3. Drain the pasta and return it to the pot.
  4. Add the tomato sauce and mix well.
  5. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese on top.
